My Design

Infographic Design

Project Title
Data Visualization Infographic
Audience

This piece was designed for a general audience seeking quick, digestible insights without sacrificing accuracy. The visual system prioritizes clarity for viewers who may not have prior knowledge of the subject, ensuring accessibility while maintaining informational depth.

The Challenge

Transform dense or data-heavy content into an intuitive format that guides the viewer through key insights without overwhelming them.

My Approach

I started by breaking the information down into its most important points and organizing it in a way that felt clear and easy to follow. I focused on creating a strong visual hierarchy so the audience could quickly understand the main ideas before diving into supporting details. Typography, spacing, and layout were used intentionally to keep the design clean and readable, allowing the information to feel approachable rather than overwhelming.
