My Design

White Bird Production Logo

Project Overview
Creating a logo for White Bird Production
My Role

Logo designer

Approach

I designed the logo to feel clean and simple while still capturing a sense of motion and creativity. The goal was to reflect the energy of a production company without overcomplicating the mark. I kept the visual language minimal, allowing subtle movement within the design to suggest storytelling and flow. Careful attention was given to typography and spacing so the logo would remain clear, flexible, and effective across both digital and print formats.
