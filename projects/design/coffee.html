My Design

Coffee Branding

Project Title
Coffee Brand Visual Identity
Audience

People who love coffee

The Challenge

Develop a distinct coffee brand in an oversaturated market while avoiding overused visual clichés. The goal was to create an identity that feels intentional and scalable rather than generic or trend-driven.

The Approach

I began by defining the brand’s personality and emotional positioning before designing any visuals. From there, I developed a cohesive identity system centered on strong typography, intentional color selection, and a flexible mark that could adapt across packaging and digital applications. The design balances clarity and character, ensuring the brand feels modern while remaining approachable.

What is the project

Brand identity and visual system for a coffee brand.
