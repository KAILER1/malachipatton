My Design

Poster Campaign

Project Overview
A speculative advertising poster promoting “Galaxy Escape 2030,” a conceptual luxury hotel experience set beyond Earth.
Audience

Designed for aspirational travelers drawn to innovation, exclusivity, and once-in-a-lifetime adventure.

The Challenge

Create a compelling advertisement for a destination that does not yet exist, while making the experience feel believable, premium, and desirable rather than purely fictional.

Approach

I approached the project as a cinematic brand launch. The expansive planetary landscape establishes scale and wonder, positioning the hotel experience as something monumental rather than ordinary. Bold, structured typography reinforces a sense of authority and futuristic confidence. The tagline, “Escape to the Stars,” anchors the concept emotionally selling transformation and transcendence instead of just accommodation. The composition emphasizes contrast between the grounded terrain and the vastness of space, reinforcing the idea of leaving Earth behind for something greater.
