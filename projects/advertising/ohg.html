OHG Campaign

Project Title
OHG Advertising Campaign
Audience

Individuals and communities seeking professional weight-loss support.

Problem

OHG (Organization for Health & Growth) needed a campaign to raise awareness and engagement. The challenge was to create visually striking materials that could cut through the noise and communicate the organization's mission in a memorable way.

Solution

Empower both patients and healthcare professionals to take proactive steps early, using medical treatments, and build a supportive environment that removes stigma and encourages action.

What is the project

An integrated advertising campaign for OHG, including poster design and promotional materials. The project combined concept development, visual design, and strategic placement to maximize reach and impact.
