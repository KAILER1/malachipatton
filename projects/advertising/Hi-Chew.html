My Design

Hi-Chew Copywritng

Project Overview
A headline-driven advertisement concept developed for Hi-Chew, focused on capturing the brand’s playful, energetic personality through concise and memorable copy.
Role

Copywriter/Designer

Challenge

Create a compelling headline and tagline that reflects the brand’s personality without advertising the product. The chalenege was to make it humorous.

Approach

I focused on developing a headline that felt playful and immediate, aligning with Hi-Chew’s vibrant tone. The goal was to create a line that could stand alone while still reinforcing the product’s unique texture and flavor experience. The tagline was designed to support the headline, strengthening brand voice and memorability. The concept centers on simplicity, clarity, and tonal alignment with the brand’s identity.
