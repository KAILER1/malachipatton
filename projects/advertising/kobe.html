Kobe

Project Title
Play for the World Campaign
Audience

Update with target audience.

Problem

The problem is global sporting events like the Olympics celebrate unity, but most sports marketing still highlights individual achievement. I wanted to shift the focus from personal glory to global impact. I want to show that when athletes compete, they represent more than themselves. They play for the world.

Solution

I solved the problem by visually integrating the globe into the moment of performance, connecting each athlete’s action directly to the world they represent. Instead of presenting athletes as isolated figures in a dramatic photo, I used selective colors such as the yellow highlight to symbolize energy extending beyond the individual athlete. By highlighting the gear, I also reinforced the product as the vehicle through which athletes compete and connect globally. The result shows that they aren’t just playing for themselves, they are playing for the world.
