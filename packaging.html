Packaging Design

A selection of packaging projects focused on product presentation.

UNO Playing Cards — Victorian-Inspired Packaging

This project reimagines the classic UNO card game through a Victorian design lens, combining ornate typography, symmetrical layouts, and rich color tones. The goal was to elevate a familiar product into a premium, collectible experience while maintaining strong brand recognition. This was hand drawn and then it was illustrated in Adobe Illustrator. Attention to detail was crucial in balancing historical aesthetics with modern readability.

Color Drift Reversible Drying Mat — Packaging Design

This packaging design was made for a company called Color drift. I wanted this project to be a nice, clean and simply design. The layout highlights key features while keeping the design clean and approachable. Emphasis was placed on legibility, product visibility, and balanced typography to create packaging that feels both practical and polished for everyday consumers.
